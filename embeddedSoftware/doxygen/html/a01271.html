Timer-triggered ADC-scan mode with DMA to memory.

Attention
Initial start of ADC conversion:
  • Once initialized, first ADC conversion must be started by a ADC_SoftwareStartConv or whereever the first channel of the regular group runs (ADC1 ,2 ,3).
  • Timer controlled group scan restart is configured by *.ADC_ExternalTrigConv = ADC_ExternalTrigConv_T2_TRGO; This means NOT that start-events for ADC regular scan are generated by the Timer update ISR!!!
  • Configure TIM for generating Output Trigger events! TIM_SelectOutputTrigger(TIM2,TIM_TRGOSource_Update);
