Detailed Description

The ASG system is designed to protect the output drivers from overload in case of actuator blocking states.

The ASG system is designed to protect the actuators and theire current drivers from overload in case of actuator blocking states. The prototype electronics also includes coil current measurement hardware to provide actuator current feedback. In a project milestone to be planned for later, the implementation of a model-driven compensator (state space model) is being considered.
